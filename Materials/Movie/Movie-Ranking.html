Animated Movies

1. Wall E

Depicts todays battle of mankind against advancements in tcehnology, like AI. It gives out a very strong message and shows us that all is never lost, we just need the strength to fight.

2. Ratatouille

This movie is bound to win your heart if you are a foodie, or just loves to watch cute human-animal interactions (even if the said animal is a rat)!

Christmas Movies

1. Home Alone

What's a boy to do when he finds himself home alone on Christmas? Maybe mess up the house? Or maybe catch theives. This movie is a great way to spend Christmas with your family through laughter and joy.

2. The Grinch

Get the milk and cookies ready, and gear up to find your inner Santa with the grumpy Grinch(is he green becaus he is jealous??). Ho! Ho! Ho! I love the emotion behind it.
